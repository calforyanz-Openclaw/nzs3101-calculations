Formulas (Clause 9.4)

Tn = Tc + Ts
Tc = 0.33 √f'c · Aoh · Ph / (3 + 0.58√f'c)
Ts = (Ao · Ath · fy · cot θ) / s
Ao ≈ 0.85 Aoh, θ = 45° for thin sections

Notes

Assumes closed rectangular stirrups.
For combined shear+torsion, use interaction per Clause 9.4.3.

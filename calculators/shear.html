Notes

This page assumes normal-weight concrete and uses vmax = min(0.2 f'c, 10 MPa). ka is linearly interpolated for aggregate sizes 10–19 mm. For dv > 400 mm, kd = (400/dv)0.25 with 0.9 ≤ kd ≤ 1.0. For dv ≤ 200 mm, vc is the larger of 0.17ka√f'c and ka vb. Results are for quick checks; confirm with the full code and design conditions.
